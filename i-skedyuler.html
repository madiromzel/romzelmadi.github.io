Key Features

Intelligent Schedule Management

  • Create academic schedules with a streamlined two-click interaction, eliminating manual time and day inputs.
  • Supports scheduling across three academic terms: 1st Semester, 2nd Semester, and Summer Semester.
  • Centralized viewing of schedules by section, professor, room, and professor working hours.

Conflict Detection and Validation

  • Built-in schedule conflict checker prevents overlapping professor assignments.
  • Stops double-booked rooms before saving changes.
  • Blocks assignments outside a professor's working hours.
  • Ensures data integrity and avoids human scheduling errors.

Role-Based Access Control

  • Admin: full system access and control.
  • Department Head: limited to assigned departments and authorized schedule creation.
  • Viewer: read-only access for reference and verification.
  • Improves security while keeping workflow accountability.

Robust Data Management

  • Full CRUD functionality across all scheduling entities.
  • Normalized relational database schema for clean updates.
  • Safe deletion without orphaned records.
  • Consistent data sync across related tables.

Reporting and Output

  • Generate print-ready and downloadable schedules.
  • Optimized layouts for readability and official reporting.

System Design Highlights

  • Modular PHP architecture for maintainability and scalability.
  • SQL-based relational database enforcing data consistency.
  • UI-driven workflow reduces administrative effort and user error.
  • Languages used: HTML, CSS, Javascript, PHP, and SQL

Why I Built It

Our department still builds schedules manually in Excel, which wastes time and makes it easy to miss conflicts. There is no built-in check for overlapping classes, no quick way to view room, section, or professor schedules, and no visibility into professor working hours. With limited rooms and a growing student population, we needed a system that maximizes room usage and makes scheduling faster, clearer, and more accurate.


With I-SKEDYULER, these challenges are addressed through an automated, system-driven approach to schedule management. The platform centralizes scheduling data, enforces real-time conflict validation, and provides clear visibility into room usage, faculty workloads, and working hours. By replacing manual spreadsheets with a structured scheduling system, I-SKEDYULER improves accuracy, reduces administrative effort, and enables faster, more reliable academic planning.
